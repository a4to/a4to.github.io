How to Use Kanban

🤚 Drag me around to move me within this list of between lists.

👆 Click me to open a modal that shows all the description, comments, files, and activity associated with this card.

🐣 Click me to see that same modal, but for a task that is brand new and doesn't have activity or files.

👇 Click "Add Card" at the bottom of this column to see what creating a new card looks like. You can even add a date.

👉 Click "Add List" past the last column on the right to see what it looks like to name a new column.

⚙️ Click the grid/list toggle in the top right of this page to see what these tasks look like organized as a draggable list!
